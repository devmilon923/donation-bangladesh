Flood at Noakhali, Bangladesh

The flood situation in Noakhali has worsened due to continuous rain and upstream water, leading many people to seek shelters. However, space is unavailable in remote areas, forcing many to live in waterlogged conditions. The flood situation further deteriorated in the district town of Maizdi and in the Sadar upazila. Meanwhile, residents have reported that relief is not properly reaching many remote areas of the district.

Flood Relief in Feni, Bangladesh

Bangladesh Coast Guard is carrying out rescue and relief operations for flood victims in Feni, it said in a press release this morning. Lieutenant Commander Sabbir Alam Sujon, media officer at the coast guard headquarters, said the coast guard was working tirelessly to combat the unprecedented flood situation. Feni's flood crisis, fueled by runoff from India and relentless rainfall, has marooned at least 3,50,000 people in three upazilas -- Fulgazi, Parshuram

Aid for Injured in the Quota Movement

Timely medical treatment can mean the difference between life and death, and whether an individual suffers permanent disability. This leads us to wonder whether the lack of financial support for treatment contributed to these tragic outcomes.
